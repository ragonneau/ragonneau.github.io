[OptiProfiler](https://github.com/OptiProfiler/OptiProfiler) is a benchmarking tool for derivative-free optimization solvers.

It is currently implemented in [Python](https://www.python.org) and [MATLAB](https://www.mathworks.com/products/matlab.html).
